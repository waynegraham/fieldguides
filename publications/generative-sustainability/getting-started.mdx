Transitioning to a generative sustainability model requires more than just technical changes; it requires an institutional shift in mindset. This section helps you assess your readiness and take the first practical steps.

## Questions to Answer

Before you begin, your team should work through these foundational questions to establish a baseline for your sustainability efforts:

- What are the core institutional values that should guide our sustainability work?
- Who are the key stakeholders (internal and external) we need to engage?
- What existing resources can we leverage or repurpose?
- How will we measure success beyond simple preservation metrics?
